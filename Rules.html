Main List

Welcome to the Main list, here you can see most the modes that people have beaten with the phonk list. The phonk playlist can be found in the rules or about section.

List Rules

1.1 List Definitions

The Main List is the Primary list on the Fnaf Phonk List. The main factor for how high a placement is on the list is the difficulty of hearing audio cues in the game over the phonk. Lets say you play a scripted night, so it's over and over again. There isn't any audio cues, since you know whats going on after. So it would be placed really low or even at the bottom.

The Unlimited List is the Secondary list on the Fnaf Phonk List, using the same placements but everything rewards points and it's less stricter than the Main List.
